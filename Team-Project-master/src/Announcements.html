COMPUTER SCIENCE DAY

11.11.2016

As is usually the case with a number of dates on the calendar February 14, connected with a historic event. On this day in 1946 the first time the Americans have shown the world the first computer - "electric Numerical Integrator and Calculator" or abbreviated as ENIAC. This huge machine was in fact an ordinary calculator to perform complex calculations of the staff of the US military service. But it was she who became the prototype of this computer, which today is in almost everyone. Computer Evolution took a very short time, in just a few decades, in the face of modern man. And all this through the merits of computer, we owe them one of the greatest inventions. Right on their professional holiday, they deserve it!
